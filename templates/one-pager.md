# One-Pager Template

Compact single-page summary for quick consumption. Used by health-score-breakdown, success-plan, meeting-prep, and any sub-skill when the user asks for "a quick summary" or "one-pager."

## Layout

```markdown
# {Account Name} — {One-Pager Title}

**Date:** {Date} | **Health:** {Score} ({Trend ↑/→/↓}) | **ARR:** ${Amount}

---

## Snapshot

| Metric | Value | Status |
|--------|-------|--------|
| {Metric 1} | {Value} | {G/Y/R or ↑/→/↓} |
| {Metric 2} | {Value} | {G/Y/R or ↑/→/↓} |
| {Metric 3} | {Value} | {G/Y/R or ↑/→/↓} |

---

## Key Findings

- {Finding 1 — cite specific data}
- {Finding 2 — cite specific data}
- {Finding 3 — cite specific data}

---

## Recommended Actions

1. **{Action}** — {Owner} — {Timeline}
2. **{Action}** — {Owner} — {Timeline}

---

*Generated by FunnelStory · {Date}*
```

## Rules

- Maximum one scroll/page. If content overflows, cut detail, not sections.
- Every finding must cite a specific number or observation from FunnelStory data.
- Recommended actions must be concrete (who does what by when), not vague ("improve engagement").
- Status indicators: use G/Y/R or trend arrows consistently across the page; define the threshold once in the Snapshot header.
- Tone: executive-scannable. Bullets, not paragraphs.
